About Me

Atmodeep, is a singer songwriter from Delhi, who has been singing and exploring the world of Music since the age of 3. He's trained in Hindustani classical but also takes inspiration from western musical influences such as Miles kennedy, Adele and Jacob Collier. His music sees a similar influence of both the worlds fused into one and believes that music is better when savoured and cherished slowly unlike today's world of catchy reel worthy hooks. Like any other engineer, he found his calling in music in the final year of his engineering course. Keeping hardwork and versatility above talent, he is trying to make an impact in music by always creating something unique and serene.
